Who is Swaggo Mayana?

Jonathan Adejo Ocheme, known as Swaggo Mayana, is an Afropop recording and performing artist recognized for his unique musical style. Originating from Ogbadibo in Benue State and raised in Abuja, Nigeria, he has a deep passion for music from a young age. His music combines African rhythms with modern pop, hip hop, and R&B influences, featuring melodious vocals, catchy lyrics, and groovy beats. Swaggo Mayana's songs often convey messages of hope, love, and resilience, resonating with his audience. He has performed at numerous events across Nigeria, collaborated with other artists, and shared the stage with top Nigerian musicians. With a growing fan base and a notable body of work, Swaggo Mayana is a prominent figure in the Nigerian music industry.
